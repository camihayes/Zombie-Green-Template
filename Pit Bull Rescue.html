Pit Bulls Are Awesome



PitBulls Are Awesome is an organization designed to help match families with the right pit bull. Animal adoption is a commitment that should be carefully thought out. Do you have small children, or elderly members in your family? Do you have cats, dogs or other animals already? What kind of outdoor space do you have? Would it be suited to a larger, older and calmer dog, or a young pup full of everlasting energy? Do you live alone? Do you travel frequently, and would you wish your pet to accompany you more often than not? Odds are, we have the perfect pitbull for you. Our step-by-step process ensures a smooth and successful adoption.

Steps to a Successful Match

Education

Our knowledgable staff at PitBulls Are Awesome will sit down with you and educate you on the responsibility of pitbull ownership, selecting a dog that is right for you and your family, and maintaining a positive experience throughout the adoption process.

View details »

Meeting

Adoption coaches will arrange two meetings. One at the rescue center, where you will have the opportunity to meet and interact with several dogs we have recommended for you based on your personal situation.

View details »

Adoption

Congratulations! We have found your perfect pitbull match! Upon a successful adoption, we will deliver the dog to your home spayed/neutered, with complete up to date shots and worming. And don't forget, we're always here to help!
